What is NFT and how can it affect the gaming industry? Quite a new technology is rapidly evolving almost every day, much that is known at the moment is quickly updated, and so it is still important to learn how it can be used. The abbreviation NFT stands for non-fungible tokens. At first glance, this designation may seem a little strange, but it is used to attach the characteristics of virtual goods, which are offered through the technology. Non-fungible tokens are an ideal means to prove ownership of any asset. Unique tokens cannot be tampered with because blockchain technology allows for their integrity to be ensured at all times.
